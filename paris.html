Paris

The city of LOVE

The city of Paris, also known as the "City of Lights", is the capital of France.
It is located on the banks of the Seine River, in the northern part of France, in the heart of the province of Il De France.
The symbol of France in general and of Paris in particular is the Eiffel Tower which has become one of the most visited sites in the city.
The tower serves as the main observation site on Paris and reaches a height of about 300 meters.
Another attraction in the city, and none of its main ones, is the Louvre, the largest palace in all of France, which is now home to one of the most impressive art and antique collections in the world.
You can also find the Tuileries Gardens, the Champs Elysees and many many other beautiful sites.
