Rome

Italian Fest!

Italy boasts one of the most beautiful and fascinating capitals in the world in general and in Europe in particular, namely Rome.
Therefore, for a traveler looking for an urban vacation - Rome is a great destination.
The city was the capital of the ancient Roman Empire, where the rulers of the empire sat and from which they conducted their affairs.
Today, Rome, sitting on the banks of the Tiber River, is the ruling center of Italy and is a global tourist attraction.
The world-famous Colosseum, has become a symbol of the city.
It is the largest structure left in the area of ​​ancient Rome and one can be impressed by its dimensions and strength even today, after all the hardships it went through.
Other attractions in the city are the famous Trevi Fountain, the Spanish Steps, Piazza Navona, the Pantheon, the museums on Capitol Hill, various churches and of course the Catholic Church on Earth, the Vatican.
A walk through the city streets, a cup of coffee in one of the charming cafes or a good Italian meal with gelatin (ice cream) for dessert - these are great pleasures in themselves, waiting for you here.
Many also leave Rome for a cruise in Europe on a destination guide.
